

 Project Overview

**BellaSpice** is a modern food and spice brand website designed to showcase premium spices, food products, and brand information. The website focuses on a clean design, easy navigation, and mobile responsiveness to provide users with a smooth browsing experience.

 Purpose

* Promote the BellaSpice brand
* Display products and services
* Provide contact and enquiry options
* Increase online visibility and customer engagement

 Technologies Used

* **HTML5**
* **CSS3**
* **JavaScript**
* **WordPress** (with Elementor)
* **PHP** (WordPress backend)
* **WooCommerce**
